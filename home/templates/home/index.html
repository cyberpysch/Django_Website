    IIT Bombay

    Get into IIT Bombay

    IIT Bombay offers admission to UG, PG, Integrated, and doctoral programmes.The admission process is both merit-based and entrance-based.The institute accepts national-level entrance exams like JEE Advanced, UCEED, GATE, CAT, CEED, IIT JAM, and its written test followed by a personal interview. JEE Advanced scores are accepted for admission to B.Tech, BS, and B.Tech M.Tech.Integrated programs admission.UCEED scores are taken into consideration for admission to the B.Des program.13 The registration for UCEED 2024 is ongoing, and the last date to apply is Oct 31, 2023. The UCEED exam 2024 will be held on Jan 21, 2024 (9 am - 12 noon). The minimum eligibility required for admission is 10+2 examination with PCM.2
